CrewLedger — Employee Receipt Tracking System

Effective Date: February 19, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Roofing & Renovations of Florida LLC ("Company," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information through the CrewLedger Receipt Tracking System ("CrewLedger" or "Service").

1. Information We Collect

When you use CrewLedger, we collect the following information:

Employee Information

  • Mobile phone number (used as your unique identifier)
  • First name and full name (provided by you during first contact)
  • Crew assignment and role (if provided)

Receipt and Purchase Data

  • Receipt photographs submitted via SMS
  • Vendor name, city, and state
  • Purchase date, subtotal, tax, and total amount
  • Payment method (cash or last four digits of card number)
  • Individual line items (item name, quantity, price)
  • Project name associated with each purchase

System Data

  • Timestamps of messages sent and received
  • Conversation state (for managing the confirmation workflow)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected through CrewLedger for the following business purposes:

  • Processing and categorizing employee expense receipts
  • Generating weekly expense reports for company accounting
  • Tracking material costs by project, vendor, and category
  • Exporting purchase data for QuickBooks bookkeeping
  • Identifying spending patterns and cost-saving opportunities
  • Sending SMS confirmation messages to verify receipt data accuracy

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

Your data may be accessed by:

  • Company management for expense oversight and approval
  • The company accountant for bookkeeping and QuickBooks entry

We use the following service providers to operate CrewLedger:

  • Twilio — for sending and receiving SMS messages
  • OpenAI — for reading receipt images (optical character recognition only; images are processed and not stored by OpenAI)

These providers process data solely to perform their service functions and are bound by their own privacy policies.

4. Data Storage and Security

All CrewLedger data is stored on local company servers. Receipt images and purchase data are not stored in third-party cloud services. We implement reasonable technical safeguards to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

5. Data Retention

We retain receipt data, images, and associated records for as long as needed for business accounting and tax compliance purposes, typically a minimum of seven (7) years in accordance with IRS record-keeping requirements. Employee records are retained for the duration of employment and a reasonable period afterward.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of the data we have collected about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data, subject to legal retention requirements
  • Opt out of SMS messages at any time by replying STOP to any CrewLedger text

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@rroflorida.com.

7. SMS Opt-Out

You may stop receiving SMS messages from CrewLedger at any time by replying STOP to any message. After opting out, you will receive a one-time confirmation that you have been unsubscribed. You will not receive any further messages unless you re-enroll.

For help, reply HELP to any CrewLedger message or contact us at info@rroflorida.com.

8. Children's Privacy

CrewLedger is a workplace tool intended for use by authorized employees only. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under the age of 18.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of CrewLedger after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
